FAQ

What type of food will be served?

There will be halal food & vegetarian options - let us know of any dietary restrictions.

What songs would you like to hear?

We encourage you to add song suggestions here.

Will there be dancing?

Yes! Dancing is a big part of both Palestinian and Bangladeshi wedding celebrations. Brush up on your dance moves before arriving. Feel free to join or start a dabke circle at any point you feel called to.

What is the dress code?

We encourage you to dress in formal, cocktail or traditional Palestinian/Bangladeshi attire.

Will there be alcohol?

No. The wedding is alcohol free and family friendly.

Will there be a seating chart?

No. Sit wherever your heart desires!

Where is the venue?

Silver Lake Country Club is in Orland Park, a suburb of Chicago which is a forty minute drive southwest of downtown (the loop). If you have a car you can stay in the city and drive. If not, a hotel in Orland Park may be more convenient. There is plenty of parking available. The celebration will be mainly indoors.

Is this running on typical Arab/Bangladeshi time?

No. Please be prompt! The festivities begin at 5pm.
